Our Commitment to Privacy

At Sweet Bakery, we value your trust and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when accessing our website, placing orders, or engaging with our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Sweet Bakery strives to ensure that all personal information is handled in a transparent and secure manner. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a delightful shopping experience and improve our services.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address, which are required to process your orders and respond to your inquiries.
  2. Payment Details: Payment information such as credit card or banking details are collected securely through third-party gateways. We do not store these details directly on our servers.
  3. Browsing Data: We may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing patterns on our website. This helps us analyze traffic and enhance the user experience.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality and personalize your shopping experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us recognize you on your next visit, save your preferences, and show you relevant products and offers.

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect certain features of our website, such as retaining your shopping cart or remembering your login details. We recommend enabling cookies to enjoy a seamless shopping experience.

How We Use Your Information

  1. To process and deliver your orders accurately and promptly, ensuring you receive freshly baked treats.
  2. To communicate with you regarding your orders, inquiries, or feedback.
  3. To send you updates about new products, special offers, or promotions if you opt to receive our marketing emails.
  4. To improve our website and services by analyzing user behavior and feedback.

Data Security

Sweet Bakery is committed to safeguarding your personal information. We use industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

While we take all necessary precautions, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. We recommend you protect your account credentials and contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized activity.

Your Rights

  1. You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information stored with us. Simply contact us, and we will assist you.
  2. You may opt out of marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or reaching out to our support team.
  3. You can request detailed information about how we use your data and who it may be shared with, if applicable.
